Your Privacy Matters

At TravelHub, we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your data.

01

Introduction

Welcome to TravelHub's Privacy Policy. This document outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information.

Who We Are

TravelHub is a travel booking platform operated by TravelHub Inc., located at 123 Travel Street, Tashkent, Uzbekistan. We are committed to transparency and your right to privacy.

By using TravelHub, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our services.

Key Points

  • We collect data to provide and improve our services
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • You have control over your data and can request deletion
  • We use industry-standard security measures
02

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide you with the best travel experience.

Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Passport details
  • Billing address

Payment Information

  • Credit/debit card details
  • PayPal account
  • Billing history
  • Transaction records

Usage Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Time on site
  • Search queries

Location Data

  • Travel destinations
  • Check-in locations

Communication Data

  • Support messages

Social Media Data

  • Profile information

  • Preferences

Sensitive Information

We collect sensitive information like passport details only when necessary for bookings. This data is encrypted and stored securely.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

Process bookings, reservations, and payments. Confirm travel arrangements and send booking confirmations.

Customer Support

Respond to inquiries, resolve issues, and provide technical assistance.

Platform Improvement

Analyze usage patterns and develop new features based on user behavior.

Marketing Communications

Send promotional offers, newsletters, and personalized recommendations.

Security & Fraud Prevention

Detect fraudulent activities and protect against security threats.

Legal Compliance

Comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests.

06

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information.

SSL/TLS Encryption

All data transmitted is encrypted using 256-bit SSL/TLS.

Encrypted Storage

Sensitive data encrypted at rest using AES-256.

Access Controls

Strict controls limit data access to authorized personnel.

Security Audits

Regular assessments and penetration testing.

Breach Notification

Users notified within 72 hours of any breach.

Backup & Recovery

Regular backups ensure data recovery.

Security Certifications

TravelHub is ISO 27001 certified and complies with SOC 2 Type II standards.

07

Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the following rights:

Right to Access

Request a copy of your personal data.

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate information.

Right to Erasure

Request data deletion.

Right to Restrict

Limit how we use your data.

Right to Portability

Receive data in structured format.

Right to Object

Object to data processing.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Privacy Team at privacy@travelhub.com. We will respond within 30 days.

08

Children's Privacy

Age Restriction

TravelHub is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Parental Notice

If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it immediately. Parents who believe their child's information has been collected should contact us at privacy@travelhub.com.

09

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own.

Where We Transfer Data

We may transfer data to servers in the United States, European Union, and other locations where our service providers operate.

Data Protection Safeguards

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

Your Consent

By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries with different data protection laws.

11

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.

Active Account

While Account is Active

We retain all your data to provide services and maintain your booking history.

After Deletion

30 Days After Account Deletion

Most personal data is deleted within 30 days of account closure.

Legal Obligations

3-7 Years for Legal Records

Transaction records and invoices kept for tax and legal compliance.

Anonymization

Anonymous Analytics Data

Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for analytics.

Deletion Requests

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting privacy@travelhub.com. Note that some data must be retained for legal compliance.

Email Notification

In-App Notification

A banner appears when you log in after policy updates.

30-Day Notice

Version History

Previous versions of the policy are archived and available upon request.

Continued Use = Acceptance

By continuing to use TravelHub after changes take effect, you agree to the updated Privacy Policy. If you disagree, please stop using our services.
