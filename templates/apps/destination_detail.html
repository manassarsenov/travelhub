With this admission ticket, you can hop aboard the iconic London Eye for a 30-minute ride over the city.

The observation wheel reaches a height of 135 metres, offering you a chance to savour panoramic views of London. You can spot famous city landmarks like Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, St Paul's Cathedral, Westminster Abbey and Trafalgar Square.

Located on the South Bank of the Thames, the London Eye is one of the most visited paid tourist attractions in the United Kingdom. Each capsule provides 360-degree views and can accommodate up to 25 passengers. During your ride, you'll slowly rotate for the full 30 minutes, giving you ample time to take in the breathtaking scenery.

This world-famous landmark offers a unique perspective of London that you simply cannot get from ground level. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a London local, the experience of seeing the city from above is truly unforgettable. The ride is fully accessible and suitable for all ages.

Why visit

  • A thrilling bird's-eye view of the English capital
  • Chance to spot prominent landmarks of the city
  • Admire Big Ben, Buckingham Palace and St Paul's Cathedral
  • Instant confirmation — no waiting in uncertainty
  • Family-friendly experience suitable for all ages

What's included

  • Admission to the London Eye
  • 30-minute capsule ride
  • Panoramic views of London's skyline

Restrictions

  • Children under 16 years old must be accompanied by an adult.
  • Visitors must show a valid photo ID.
  • Not recommended for people with vertigo or severe acrophobia.
  • Wheelchair accessible — please notify in advance.

Additional information

Please note that the time slot is for when you enter the queue for the attraction, not the time you are to gain admission. Holiday periods are subject to longer wait times.

Please note that attractions may close areas or exhibits at their discretion on the date of your visit. For all ticket holders, the number of people per capsule may vary.

There are strict security procedures at the London Eye. Please note that visitors are required to undergo a non-invasive security check before boarding. You can request to be searched by an officer of the same sex.

The following items are not permitted: alcohol, baseball bats, bicycles (including folding ones), explosives (including fireworks), guns, knives, pepper spray, pets (except for service animals), prams and buggies, sharp objects, scooters, skateboards, tasers and tripods.
